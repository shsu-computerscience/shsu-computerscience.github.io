Cryptography

Foci

    Learn
  • Cryptocurrencies (such as basic proof-of-work)
  • Homomorphic encryption (such as simple homomorphic cipers (RSA, ElGamal, Pailler, Goldwasser-Micali)
  • Elliptical curves (such as elliptical curve ElGamal, Diffie-Hellman)
  • Zero-knowledge proofs (such as Schnorr signatures, ZKPs of graph theoretic properties (isomorphism, non-isomorphism))
  • Novel cryptography
    Do not learn
  • Deep knowledge of algorithms for underlying symmetric primitives (block ciphers, pseudorandom number generators)
  • Design or implementation of cryptographic primitives or protocols
  • Multiparty or distributed protocols.
