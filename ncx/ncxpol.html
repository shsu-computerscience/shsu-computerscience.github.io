Policy

The [policy] module focuses on public policy, which in the United States is quite complex. This part of the competition begins with the judges providing you with a fictional scenario where a large government contractor was hacked. The implications of the hack were massive, and it took an in-depth review of multiple legal documents and federal policies to navigate best on how we should recommend to the president to respond.
